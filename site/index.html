    How to play

    A cryptogram is a message in which all the letters are scrambled. Your goal is to figure out the original message.

    To fill a guess, click on the corresponding tile and type your guess. When you fill all the tiles, you will know whether you got the quote correctly.

    If you have a physical keyboard: use arrow keys, Home, End, Ctrl + Left/Right and Shift + Left/Right to navigate more efficiently.
